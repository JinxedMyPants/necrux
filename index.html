About Necrux

Necrux is more than a guild—we're a brotherhood forged in the fires of competitive Wild Rift. Founded on principles of excellence, teamwork, and relentless improvement, we dominate the rift through superior macro play, mechanical precision, and unwavering coordination.

Our players are handpicked from the top ranks, each bringing unique strengths and champion mastery. We compete in regional and international tournaments, continuously pushing the boundaries of what's possible in mobile MOBA esports.
